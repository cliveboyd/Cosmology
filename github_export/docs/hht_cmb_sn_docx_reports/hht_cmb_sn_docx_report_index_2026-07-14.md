## Notable Extracted Findings

- BAO-HHT integration reports record a sign shift in the preferred BAO DM-DH covariance parameter between BAO-only and SN-anchored fits.
- Several reports emphasize IMF2/IMF3 locking tests against BAO scale proxies, with candidate locking around the SN/BAO residual structure.
- The SNIA BAO dechirp reports document chirp/dechirp-style HHT treatments of supernova residuals and BAO-aligned features.
- The January 2026 HHT status reports preserve the later robustness framing, including seed-grid and seasonal/phase tests.
- The cosmology equations report explicitly includes CMB distance-prior definitions as part of the locking formalism.
