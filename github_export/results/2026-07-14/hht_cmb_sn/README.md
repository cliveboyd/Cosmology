# HHT CMB/SN Results Capture

This folder captures small historical and current HHT outputs relevant to CMB/SN/combined-probe residual screening.

Tracked files include:

- modern SN and extended real-data HHT residual exports;
- HHT IMF summaries for SU2/SU2R/LCDM residual comparisons;
- shuffled-order surrogate controls;
- older SN/BAO IMF-lock summaries;
- `hht_bridge` posterior-link and robustness summaries.

Large MCMC chains, `.npy` arrays, `.npz` details, plots, logs, and Office reports are intentionally excluded.
